RAGFlow lets you edit the ingestion and retrieval flow as a graph of nodes. The dataset synced from AutoRAG appears as a pre-wired flow; you can add, remove, or reconfigure nodes (parser, chunker, embedding, retrieval, LLM).

What you can do in the flow editor

  • Reorder or replace nodes — e.g. add a Re-ranker between Retrieval and LLM, or switch the parser to a different document type.
  • Edit node config — Click a node (e.g. Chunker) to change chunk_token_num, delimiter, or embedding model in the right-hand panel.
  • Wire new nodes — Drag from the palette onto the canvas and connect inputs/outputs; RAGFlow validates the flow before save.
  • Use with AutoRAG — The flow you deploy from AutoRAG becomes the initial graph; further edits in RAGFlow stay in RAGFlow until you re-deploy from AutoRAG (overwrite) or keep both in sync manually.
