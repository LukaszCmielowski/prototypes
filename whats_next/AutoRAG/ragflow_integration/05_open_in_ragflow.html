Why open in RAGFlow?

  • Refine chunking — Tweak chunk_token_num, delimiter, or switch to Q&A / Paper template without re-running AutoRAG.
  • Add documents — Upload more files in RAGFlow; they are parsed with the current (AutoRAG-synced or customized) config.
  • Change embedding model — Use a different model available in RAGFlow; re-index if needed.
  • Create chats/agents — In RAGFlow UI, create a Chat or Agent that uses this dataset, then share or embed.
